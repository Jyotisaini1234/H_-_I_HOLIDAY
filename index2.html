About Us

U & I Holidays is a Destination Management Company for Indonesia.

Established in 2009, we yearn persistently to offer the best of the services in a coordinated way for our Travel Trade partners, so that they in turn drive their retail business by offering phenomenal experiences to the end users. Conceptualized by two budding entrepreneurs Shyam Upadhyay & Ashish Indulkar, we assure comprehensive ground handling solutions including viable logistics, prompt local assistance and meticulous resource management to outline a spectacular experience for the discerning traveller.

Vision: To be the Best Service Provider in the travel industry, by blending technology & human efforts for prompt service delivery.

Values: Honesty & Integrity are the virtues that we strongly believe in & will never compromise them under any circumstances.
